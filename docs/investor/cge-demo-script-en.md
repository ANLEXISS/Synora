# Presenter script — 8 to 10 minutes

Use the same ten chapters as the French script. The on-screen language is
switched locally and the seed, policies and results do not change.

1. Reactive systems: an event does not have the same meaning in every home.
2. Context: the engine adds where, when, domestic state and context quality.
3. Memory: repeated observations form local chains and routines after warm-up.
4. Ambiguity: equal candidates remain hypotheses; nothing is selected silently.
5. Deviation: a bounded explanatory index is decomposed into structural,
   temporal and interval factors.
6. Adaptation: legitimate schedule change becomes history over successive
   observations; the intentionally fast adaptation still needs field calibration.
7. Replay: the durable digest is equal before and after controlled demo restart;
   the deviation store is ephemeral by design.
8. Local-first: the demonstrated learning path requires no cloud; do not claim
   that every Synora ecosystem communication is absent.
9. Differentiation: contextual graph, hypotheses, versioned memory, routines,
   explainability and transactional resilience work together.
10. Real status: show `synthetic_episode_not_separated`, state what is proven,
    what remains to prove, and that security authority remains future.

For technical questions, switch to Technical mode and use “View evidence”.
